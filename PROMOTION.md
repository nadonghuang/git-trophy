# 🏆 git-trophy

Analyze your git repo and earn achievement trophies — gamify your contributions!

Ever wondered what kind of developer you are? Night owl or early bird? Weekend warrior or Monday champion? git-trophy scans your commit history and awards you 23 unique trophies across 5 rarity tiers.

🔑 Key features: Zero deps, Python 3.8+, beautiful terminal UI, JSON output, 23 trophies from Common to Legendary.

⭐ If you've ever committed code at 3 AM just to get that "Night Owl" trophy... this one's for you.
